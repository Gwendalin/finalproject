Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26/11/2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and email address (if you sign up for our services).
  • Data from your Pinterest account, including:
    • Public boards, pins, and their metadata (e.g., titles, descriptions, and images).
    • User interactions, such as saved pins or liked content.

Non-Personal Information

  • Device information, including IP address and browser type.
  • Usage data, such as app activity logs and interaction patterns.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Pinterest Data:
    • To analyze user preferences and provide tailored fashion recommendations based on Pinterest boards and pins.
    • To allow users to save designs or inspiration to their Pinterest boards.
  • Enhancing User Experience:
    • To personalize content and improve the app's functionality.
  • Communication:
    • To send notifications, updates, and relevant design recommendations.

3. Pinterest API Compliance

Our application uses Pinterest's API to access data from your Pinterest account with your explicit consent. We adhere to Pinterest's API Terms of Service and only use the data to provide the stated functionality, such as generating personalized fashion recommendations. Your Pinterest data is:

  • Accessed only after obtaining your consent through a secure OAuth authentication process.
  • Used exclusively for the purposes outlined in this policy, such as providing tailored recommendations and integrating with your boards.
  • Not stored persistently beyond 30 days or the duration of the specific task.
  • Not shared with third parties except as required by law or to provide essential services (e.g., hosting or analytics).

You can revoke our access to your Pinterest data at any time by disconnecting your Pinterest account through our app or by contacting us.

4. How We Secure Your Data

  • Data Encryption: All data transmitted between our app and Pinterest is encrypted using HTTPS to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to user data stored on our servers.
  • Retention Period:
    • Pinterest data is cached temporarily during user sessions and is deleted automatically within 30 days.

5. User Consent and Opt-Out

We value your privacy and ensure that your data is accessed only with your explicit consent. When you connect your Pinterest account to our app, you will be redirected to Pinterest for secure authentication.

If you wish to disconnect your Pinterest account or revoke data access, you can do so at any time through:

  • Account settings within the app.
  • Contacting our support team at gwendalincy-pm21@student.tarc.edu.my.

6. Use of Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Enhance your user experience by saving preferences.
  • Analyze how users interact with the app to improve functionality.

Users can manage their cookie preferences through browser settings.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Updates will be posted with a revised "Effective Date."
